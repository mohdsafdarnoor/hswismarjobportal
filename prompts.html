AI Prompt Library

Structured Prompts for Career Success

How to Use These Prompts: Replace placeholders in [brackets] with your specific information. Copy the complete prompt into ChatGPT or similar AI tools. Always personalize the AI-generated output before using it in applications.

Internships in Germany

Mandatory Internship (No Experience)

Act as a German university career advisor.

I am an international master's student and must complete a mandatory internship (Pflichtpraktikum).

My background:

  • Degree and specialization: [Your degree]
  • Relevant coursework: [Your coursework]
  • Tools or software I know: [Your tools]
  • German language level: [A1/A2/B1/B2/C1]

Task:

  1. Suggest suitable mandatory internship roles in Germany
  2. Convert my coursework and projects into CV-ready experience
  3. Create a one-page German-style CV suitable for a Pflichtpraktikum
  4. Suggest interview questions that German recruiters might ask

Voluntary Internship (No Experience)

You are a German recruiter hiring voluntary interns.

I am applying for a voluntary internship (freiwilliges Praktikum) with no prior work experience.

Task:

  1. Identify what recruiters realistically expect from voluntary interns
  2. Rewrite my CV to emphasize learning ability and reliability
  3. Draft a short cover letter explaining why a voluntary internship makes sense

Internship With Prior Experience

Act as a German hiring manager.

I am applying for an internship and already have prior experience in my field.

My experience: [Describe your experience]

Task:

  1. Help me position myself as a high-impact intern rather than a beginner
  2. Rewrite my CV bullets to show contribution, not just learning
  3. Suggest how to negotiate better responsibilities or compensation

Working Student Jobs (Werkstudent)

Werkstudent (No Experience)

Act as an HR recruiter in Germany hiring Werkstudent roles.

I have no prior work experience.

My background:

  • Degree and field of study: [Your degree]
  • Current semester: [Your semester]
  • Weekly availability: [Your hours]

Task:

  1. Suggest realistic Werkstudent roles I can apply for
  2. Rewrite my CV to highlight academic and transferable skills
  3. Prepare answers for the question "Do you have practical experience?"

Werkstudent (Early Experience)

You are a German HR Business Partner.

I have early professional experience and am applying for a Werkstudent role.

My experience: [Describe 0-2 years experience]

Task:

  1. Align my experience with German market expectations
  2. Rewrite my CV bullets using impact-oriented language
  3. Draft a short professional email to HR

Career Shift (No German Experience)

Act as a career transition coach in Germany.

I am changing from [previous role] to [new role] with no German work experience.

Task:

  1. Identify transferable skills relevant to the new role
  2. Rewrite my CV to reduce perceived hiring risk
  3. Prepare interview answers explaining the career shift convincingly

Full-Time Jobs

Full-Time Job Application

Act as a German recruiter hiring graduates for full-time roles.

I have relevant experience and will graduate in [month and year].

My background:

  • Degree: [Your degree]
  • Experience: [Your experience]
  • Target role: [Your target]

Task:

  1. Rewrite my CV to position me as job-ready
  2. Optimize the CV for German ATS systems
  3. Draft a professional German-style cover letter

Interview Preparation

Conduct a mock interview for a full-time role in Germany.

Role: [Your target role]

Company type: [Startup/Corporate/SME]

After each answer, evaluate:

  • Structure
  • Cultural fit
  • Clarity

Also explain what German interviewers value in strong answers.

Onboarding Preparation

Act as an HR onboarding coach in Germany.

I am joining my first full-time job in Germany.

Role: [Your role]

Create a 30-60-90 day plan covering:

  • Performance expectations
  • Communication norms
  • Cultural dos and don'ts

Master's Thesis

Finding Companies for Thesis Collaboration

Act as a German industry-academia collaboration advisor.

I want to complete my Master's thesis in collaboration with a company.

My details:

  • Degree: [Your degree]
  • Specialization: [Your focus]
  • Preferred industry: [Your industry]

Task:

  1. Identify types of companies open to thesis students
  2. Suggest departments where Master's theses are common
  3. List platforms and strategies to find such companies

Convincing Company to Allow Thesis

You are a German hiring manager.

I want to combine a working student role with my Master's thesis.

Task:

  1. Frame my thesis as a business solution rather than an academic burden
  2. Draft a convincing email to HR or the hiring manager
  3. Address concerns about time commitment, confidentiality, and supervision

Defining Thesis Topic

Propose three Master's thesis topics aligned with:

  • My degree: [Your degree]
  • The company's business challenges: [Company type/industry]

For each topic, explain:

  • Business value
  • Expected outcome
  • Low risk for the company

Common Thesis and Job Problems

Act as a student advisor in Germany.

Help me handle the following situations:

  • The company wants work output but does not recognize the thesis
  • Conflicts between university requirements and company expectations
  • Supervisor availability or support issues
