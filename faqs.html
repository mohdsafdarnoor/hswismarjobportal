FAQs

Clear, practical answers for using AI tools responsibly in the German job market.

Can I use AI to write my CV and cover letter?

Yes—use AI for structure, grammar, and brainstorming, but keep the content truthful and specific to your experience.

How do I avoid “AI-sounding” applications?

Remove generic adjectives, add concrete proof points, and rewrite final text in your own voice before submitting.

Should I apply in German or English?

Follow the job advertisement language first. If the job ad is German, a German application is usually expected.

What should I prepare for interviews in Germany?

Prepare a 60–90 second introduction, 5 proof points with numbers, and a list of questions about the team, tasks, and next steps.

Where can I report broken links or suggest tools?

Use the contact form to report issues or send suggestions. Go to Contact.
