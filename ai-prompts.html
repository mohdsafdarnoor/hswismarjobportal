AI Prompts Library

Ready-to-use ChatGPT prompts for your career journey in Germany

How to use these prompts: Copy the entire prompt structure, replace the placeholders with your specific information, and paste it into ChatGPT or similar AI tools. Always personalize the AI's output before using it in your applications.

Important: These prompts are templates. The AI's responses should be starting points, not final outputs. Add your personal experiences, specific examples, and authentic voice to all materials.

Working Student / Part-Time Jobs

Prompts for finding and applying to Werkstudent positions in Germany

Werkstudent / Part-Time Role – No Experience

Background Information to Provide:

  • Degree and field of study
  • University and current semester
  • Relevant coursework or academic projects
  • Tools, software, or methods familiar with
  • German and English language level
  • Weekly availability (hours)

Copy-Paste Prompt:

I am an international student looking for a Werkstudent/part-time job in Germany with no prior work experience. Here is my background: - Degree: [Your degree, e.g., Master's in International Management] - University: [Your university] - Current Semester: [e.g., 2nd semester] - Relevant Coursework: [List 3-4 relevant courses] - Academic Projects: [Describe 1-2 key projects] - Tools/Software: [List tools you know, e.g., Excel, PowerPoint, etc.] - German Language Level: [e.g., A2, B1] - English Language Level: [e.g., C1, fluent] - Weekly Availability: [e.g., 15-20 hours] Please help me with the following tasks: 1. Suggest 5-7 realistic Werkstudent roles suitable for someone with no prior work experience 2. Explain which roles are typically open to international students in Germany 3. Rewrite my CV to highlight academic strengths and transferable skills 4. Prepare strong answers to the question: "Do you have practical experience?" 5. Advise how to position myself as reliable and trainable to German employers

Remember: Use Prompts Responsibly

These prompts are designed to help you start conversations with AI tools and organize your thoughts. They are not meant to generate final, ready-to-submit documents. Always:

  • Personalize outputs with your specific experiences and authentic voice
  • Verify information for accuracy and relevance to your situation
  • Add specific examples from your own background
  • Review and edit everything before using it
  • Seek human feedback from career advisors or mentors when possible
