Use AI responsibly

AI should improve your quality and speed, but your application must stay truthful and consistent across CV, cover letter, and interviews.

  • Use AI for structure, grammar, and brainstorming.
  • Add concrete proof (numbers, outcomes, links).
  • Never claim skills or experience you cannot explain.
