Important: Use AI responsibly

AI should support your job search—not replace your work. Many companies reject applications that look fully automated.

  • Use AI for structure, grammar, and brainstorming.
  • Add your real experience (projects, coursework, measurable results).
  • Verify every claim before submitting an application.
