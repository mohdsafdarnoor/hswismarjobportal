Recommended workflow

Target → Tailor → Prepare

  • Choose 1–2 target roles and employers.
  • Align your CV and cover letter with the job description.
  • Prepare role questions, STAR stories, and a short self‑introduction.
!

Use AI without losing credibility

AI can support your language and structure, but applications that look fully automated are easy to recognise.

  • Write in your own voice and keep all details verifiable.
  • Prefer short sentences and concrete evidence over adjectives.
  • Never claim experience you cannot explain in an interview.
